Korean Ground Beef Bowl

This is a quick and easy recipe for anybody on the go.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 5 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon freshly grated ginger
  • 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 6 green onions, chopped, divided
  • 4 cups hot cooked brown rice
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add beef and cook for 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Stir in garlic, ginger, and sesame oil and cook until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in soy sauce, brown sugar, and red pepper. Cook for additional 5 minutes.
  4. Add 1/2 of chopped green onions.
  5. Serve over hot cooked rice; garnish with sesame seeds and green onions.
