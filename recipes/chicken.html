Chicken Cordon Bleu

An easy and delicious chicken recipe that could be made in under an hour.

Ingredients

  • nonstick cooking sparay
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 6 slices of swiss cheese
  • 4 slices cooked ham
  • 1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Coat baking dish with cooking spray.
  3. Pound chicken breasts to 1/4 thickness.
  4. Sprinkle each piece of chicken with salt and pepper.
  5. Place 1 piece of ham and cheese on top of each breast.
  6. Roll up each breast and secure with toothpicks.
  7. Place in baking dish and sprinkle evenly with bread crumbs.
  8. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink inside.
  9. Remove from the oven and place 1/2 slice of cheese on top.
  10. Return to oven for 3 to 5 minutes, remove toothpicks and serve immediately.
