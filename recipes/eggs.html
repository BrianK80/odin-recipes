Fluffy Microwave Scrambled Eggs

A quick and easy recipe for a quick breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Break eggs into a microwave safe bowl and add salt, mix well.
  3. Microwave on high power fro 30 seconds. Remove bowl and beat eggs very well and return bowl to the microwave for another 30 seconds.
  4. Repeat this pattern for up to 2 1/2 minutes.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy
